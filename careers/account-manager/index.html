MadeByShape are looking for an Account Manager.

We'd like a friendly individual to come in and look after our team in-house, and our clients (all over the world). Organisation is key, and communication skills need to be super.

We're on our way to creating a stress-free environment, and a big part of that is exceptional organisation and communication skills. A friendly person to look after every project, and the people involved.

This position will directly support the MadeByShape team, and the multiple projects we deliver for a range of clients.

Main duties & responsibilities include:

Calendar Organisation
Excellent Communication Skills (face to face, by phone, and by email)
Communicating with designers and developers to make sure they are on schedule
Communicating with clients to request info the MadeByShape need
Join phone calls, email and zoom meetings with clients to listen to specific requests
Manage your own workload and maintain individual and team KPIs
Assisting the team with content strategy
Building and maintaining strong, long-lasting customer relationships
Operating as the lead point of contact for a range of projects
Develop trusted advisor relationships with key accounts
Clearly communicate the progress of monthly/quarterly initiatives

And if you want to know what it's like to work at MadeByShape. Ella Dawson (Brand Designer) - our latest team member, has published an article on what she's learnt in the first 6 months of being here.

What we need to see:

Excellent writing and grammar skills
Ability to have a good sense of humour
Must speak native English
Excellent time management and communication skills
Must be UK based (preferably North West)
Experience at a web design agency is ideal but not necessary

Benefits of working at MadeByShape

Minimum 2 week holiday at christmas
Work from home (or the studio)
Regular team social events
Early Friday Finish
Cycle to Work Scheme
Performance related bonuses
Potential to earn commission
Flexible hours
Macbook Pro
Training & Development
Option to bring your pet to the studio
Mobile Phone (After 1 Year)
Spotify Subscription
We only choose to work with nice clients

How to Apply:

So if you think you've got what it takes and this role is perfect for you, we'd love to hear from you.

*We are happy to discuss this opportunity as Part-Time, Full-Time or on a Contract basis.*

During the current climate, we will be asking specific candidates to attend a zoom interview.
